Frequently Asked Questions

How does QuickDrop work?

QuickDrop monitors your clipboard for new screenshots. When detected, it automatically uploads the image to Cloudflare R2 cloud storage, generates a short shareable link, and replaces the screenshot in your clipboard with the link.

Is QuickDrop really free?

Yes! QuickDrop is 100% free forever with no file limits or paywalls. The infrastructure costs less than $1/month using Cloudflare's free tier, making it sustainable to offer for free.

What happens after 24 hours?

All screenshots are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours to protect your privacy. This ensures no permanent storage of your content.

Do I need to create an account?

Nope! No account, no email, no signup required. Just download, install, and start sharing screenshots instantly.

Is QuickDrop open source?

Yes! QuickDrop is fully open source under the MIT license. You can view the code, contribute improvements, or even self-host your own instance.

Which platforms are supported?

QuickDrop works on macOS (Intel and Apple Silicon), Windows, and Linux. The shareable links work on all platforms and devices.
